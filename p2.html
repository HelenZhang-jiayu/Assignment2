PAST
You said it is hard to leave the past,
But new journey weren’t that bad.
Apparently it still has a path that leads to the end,
But the journey seems to be ended.
You grabbed me out of the abyss,
And convinced me to leave my new past.
Your promise pledges in a tale
And there won’t be excitements for bed time story.
You raised me upon the sky,
And I saw brilliant kites
But no one fight through soreness
And your arms bend eventually.
You said you love me no more,
But no one can handle it and they all mourn.
Everything starts like a dream and I woke up in a sudden drop,
Light shine no more,
Desperation died for more.
I fell in a deeper root,
May be delighted no more.
